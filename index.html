About Lotus Polyclinic

Lotus Polyclinic, at Ponmar, Chennai, built upon the foundation of Sri Sai Clinic, established in 2020, with a vision to provide quality and affordable healthcare to the community. Guided by compassion and commitment, we have grown into a multi-specialty clinic dedicated to patient-centered care.

At Lotus Polyclinic, we strive to combine medical expertise with empathy, ensuring that every individual receives comprehensive, evidence-based, and personalized treatment.

Compassion

Healthcare as service, not privilege. We treat every patient with empathy and kindness.

Accessibility

Easy reach for underserved populations. Quality healthcare should reach everyone.

Trust & Expertise

Led by a female doctor with specializations in diabetology, obstetrics, gynecology, and general medicine.

Modern Care

Multi-department facility with visiting specialists and comprehensive healthcare services.

Our Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to exceptional healthcare

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To deliver uncompromised, expert healthcare to all, grounded in compassion and guided by the best of modern medical excellence.

Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to every patient who walks through our doors, treating each individual with dignity, empathy, and respect.

We are committed to:

  • Delivering evidence-based medical care that meets the highest standards of clinical excellence
  • Fostering genuine human connections through compassionate listening and personalized attention
  • Creating a healing environment where patients feel heard, valued, and empowered in their health journey

We believe that exceptional healthcare is both a science and an art—combining medical expertise with genuine care for the whole person. Every patient deserves our best, and we pledge to deliver nothing less.

Our Facilities

We offer comprehensive healthcare services to meet all your medical needs under one roof.

General Medicine

Comprehensive primary care for all ages, from routine check-ups to chronic disease management.

Diabetology

Specialized care for diabetes management, including monitoring, education, and complication screening.

Women's Health

Complete OB/GYN services, prenatal care, family planning, and menopause management.

Dermatology

Diagnosis and treatment of skin conditions, allergies, and minor dermatological procedures.

Pharmacy

Full-service in-house pharmacy with medication counseling and prescription management.

Diagnostic Lab

On-site laboratory for quick and reliable blood tests, urine analysis, and other diagnostics.
